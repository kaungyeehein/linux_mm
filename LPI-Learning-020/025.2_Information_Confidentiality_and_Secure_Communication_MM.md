# 025.2 Information Confidentiality and Secure Communication
## (သတင်းအချက်အလက်လျှို့ဝှက်မှုနှင့် လုံခြုံသော ဆက်သွယ်ရေး)

---

## Data Leaks & Interception
- Unencrypted communication ထဲက data ကို intercept လုပ်နိုင်
- Leak ဖြစ်ရင် financial + legal + reputational impact

---

## NDAs (Non-Disclosure Agreements)
- Confidential info မထုတ်ဖော်ရန် စာချုပ်
- Culture + controls နဲ့တွဲပြီးသာထိရောက်

---

## Information Classification
- Public
- Internal
- Confidential
- Highly Confidential
- Classification အလိုက် access control/encryption/retention ချ

---

## Secure Communication Practices
- Email encryption (OpenPGP/S/MIME)
- Secure messaging (E2EE concept)
- Secure file sharing: permission control + link expiry (concept)
- Verify recipient identity

---

## Exam Focus
- NDAs idea
- Classification levels
- Secure communication basics
