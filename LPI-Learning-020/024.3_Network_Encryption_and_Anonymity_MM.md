# 024.3 Network Encryption and Anonymity
## (Network လျှို့ဝှက်ရေးနှင့် အမည်မဖော်မှု)

---

## VPN
- Encrypted tunnel တည်ဆောက်ပြီး traffic ကို ကာကွယ်
- Use cases: remote access, site-to-site, public Wi‑Fi protection
- Limitation: provider trust (public VPN)

---

## Transfer Encryption vs End-to-End
- Transfer (in transit): HTTPS/VPN tunnel
- E2EE: sender↔receiver only

---

## Anonymity Concepts
- IP/MAC tracking ဖြစ်နိုင်
- Tools: VPN, proxy, Tor
- “အပြည့်အဝအမည်မဖော်” ခက်ခဲ (fingerprinting, logs)

---

## Proxy Servers
- Forward proxy: client→internet
- Reverse proxy: protect/load-balance servers
- Risk: proxy logs, no encryption by default

---

## Tor / Darknet (Concept)
- Multi-hop routing နဲ့ anonymity တိုး
- .onion services

---

## Exam Focus
- VPN concept
- proxy vs VPN vs Tor
- transfer vs E2EE
