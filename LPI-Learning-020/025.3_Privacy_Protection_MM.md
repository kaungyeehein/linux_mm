# 025.3 Privacy Protection
## (ကိုယ်ရေးကိုယ်တာလုံခြုံရေး/Privacy ကာကွယ်ခြင်း)

---

## Personal Information Importance
- Personal info ပေါက်ကွဲရင် identity theft, stalking, scam အန္တရာယ်တက်

---

## Risks of Publishing Personal Info
- Social engineering/phishing တိုး
- Profiling/targeted ads

---

## Rights & Regulations (Concept)
- GDPR-like principles: informed, access, correction, deletion rights (idea level)

---

## Tracking & Profiling
- Cookies, trackers, fingerprinting
- Mitigation:
  - browser privacy settings
  - block third-party cookies
  - ad/script blockers
  - careful app permissions

---

## Managing Privacy Settings
- Social media privacy controls
- Limit public posts, review friend lists, location sharing off

---

## Exam Focus
- Tracking mechanisms
- Privacy settings management
- Personal info sharing risk

---

## Quick Check
1. Cookie tracking က ဘာလဲ?
2. Personal info အွန်လိုင်းတင်ခြင်းက ဘာကြောင့်အန္တရာယ်ရှိလဲ?
