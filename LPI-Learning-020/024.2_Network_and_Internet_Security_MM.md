# 024.2 Network and Internet Security
## (Network/Internet လုံခြုံရေး)

---

## Link Layer Risks
- Packet sniffing
- Man-in-the-middle (MITM)
- ARP spoofing (concept)

---

## Wi‑Fi Security
- Open Wi‑Fi = interception risk
- WEP (weak) → WPA2/WPA3 (stronger)
- Strong password + disable WPS (best practice)

---

## DoS/DDoS
- Service ကို မရနိုင်အောင် traffic flood
- Botnets နဲ့ DDoS လုပ်နိုင်
- Mitigation: rate limiting, filtering, upstream protection

---

## Packet Filters / Firewalls
- Rule-based traffic allow/deny
- Attack mitigation အတွက် အခြေခံကိရိယာ

---

## Exam Focus
- MITM concept
- DDoS + botnet
- firewall/packet filter role
